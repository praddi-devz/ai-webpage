What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence or AI is a technology which helps computers to do work that normally needs human thinking. It can learn from data and solve different types of problems. Nowadays AI is used in many places around us.

Quick Facts

Full Form: Artificial Intelligence

Field: Computer Science

Used In: Healthcare, Education, Banking, Transport, Agriculture

Main Goal: To make machines perform intelligent tasks.

Interesting Facts About AI

  • AI can play games like Chess and Go.
  • Some robots use AI to communicate with people.
  • AI is used by space agencies to analyse large amounts of data.
  • Many smartphones use AI for face unlock.

AI in Everyday Life

  • Voice assistants like Siri and Google Assistant.
  • Google Maps finding the fastest route.
  • YouTube recommending videos.
  • Netflix suggesting movies.
  • Email spam filters.

Some Real Life Applications

Healthcare

Doctors use AI to detect diseases and study medical reports more quickly.

Education

Students can use AI for learning new topics and getting help in studies

Banking

AI helps banks to find fraud and keep customer accounts more secure.

Transportation

Navigation apps and some modern cars use AI for better travelling.


Shopping

Shopping websites suggest products based on what people search.

Agriculture

Farmers use AI to check crop health and improve farming.

Advantages

  • Saves Time.
  • Can do work faster.
  • Reduces human mistakes.
  • Helpful in many different fields

Disadvantages

  • Can replace human jobs.
  • Costs alot to develop.
  • Sometimes gives wrong answers.
  • May be biased to a specific group

Conclusion

Artificial Intelligence is becoming more important every year. It is helping people in education, hospitals, farming and many other areas. AI has many benefits but it should be used carefully so that it helps everyone.
